Hamburger

A classic hamburger is a simple yet delicious sandwich consisting of a ground meat patty, typically beef, served on a bun with various toppings and condiments.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the hamburgers by placing the patties on the bottom buns and adding your desired toppings.
